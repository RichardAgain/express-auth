A Look at Sonic The Hedgehog His World
Since he entered the battle against injustice, Sonic has been the winner of peace and is recognized on Earth for having saved it countless times. During his many adventures, Sonic has traveled from the ends of the world to the ends of space and time, facing countless tests that have tested him to the max, earning him many titles, allies, and the scorn of several enemies. Known for his legendary arrogant, calm attitude and somewhat short temper, but with a strong sense of justice, compassion and love of freedom and adventure, Sonic uses his abilities to protect the innocents of his world and the world beyond. beyond the forces of evil, especially his archenemy, Dr. Eggman, and his empire. Sonic is described as being "equal to the wind"; a relaxed person, with a free and adventurous spirit. He considers freedom to be part of everything, living his life his way without obeying the standards presented to him and what he enjoys most is traveling the world, never thinking twice about what people say or think to him. Hardened with a strong passion for life, he is always looking for new experiences, making his life endless adventures. Due to his need to be free, Sonic dislikes being locked up without being able to move, such as in Sonic Adventure 2 where he could not stay calm while locked in a small prison. Likewise, he tends to be too restless and hates waiting too long, a detail that is represented in his idle animations. Although it is unknown if he has received any training, Sonic's years of battles have made him a skilled and powerful fighter. He has defeated giant robots, demons, and gods by himself, faced and bested those with far more combat experience, and even defeated people who are equal in their abilities. It has also been noted that Sonic is constantly getting stronger; during the Time Eater Incident, Sonic was able to defeat the Egg Emperor/Perfect Chaos on his own, when he previously required additional power. Sonic even told Infinite when defending against the latter compared to the previous time they fought that he becomes more powerful every second. It's safe to say that Sonic is much more powerful than he lets on, but his overconfidence and reluctance to go all out usually prevents him from fighting at full strength. His victories over Emerl in Gizoid's full potential and Perfect Chaos in his base form are prime examples of Sonic's true power. Regardless of his blocks, Sonic is among the most powerful characters in the series. Sonic's greatest strength is his super speed, an innate talent possessed by members of the hedgehog species. Sonic, however, has proven himself in a class of his own with his speed, having been credited as "the fastest being alive" and even "the fastest being in the universe." It has the ability to instantly accelerate to the speed of sound and run at speeds much greater than the Mach 1, which is at the blistering speed of approximately 768 mph, and it can run backwards as well as it can go forward. Because of that speed, he can climb vertical surfaces and ceilings, run over water, outrun lasers, and even make the ground explode in his wake. Even then, Sonic can push his speed even further. By accelerating in place, you can take off at turbo speeds in an instant. He can also envelop himself in a blue energy field that allows him to accelerate beyond the sound barrier on the ground and in the air for a short time, allowing him to not only directly pass through enemies and obstacles like a live projectile, but also fly immense distances through the air. Using certain techniques, he can even reach the speed of light momentarily.
